About Batik Kitchen

Founded in 2010, Batik Kitchen brings the authentic flavors of Indonesia to your table. Our chefs are trained in traditional Indonesian cooking techniques and use only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that transport you to the streets of Jakarta, Bali, and beyond.

Our menu features regional specialties from across the Indonesian archipelago, from the spicy rendang of West Sumatra to the sweet and savory satay of Java. Each dish is prepared with care and attention to detail, honoring the rich culinary heritage of our homeland.

At Batik Kitchen, we believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's a celebration of culture, family, and community. We invite you to join us for an unforgettable culinary journey through Indonesia.
